About us

After a scare using Facebook Marketplace his freshman year of College, We have the idea for a college ONLY marketplace. This would be a platform where students would not have to worry about dangerous individuals or scammers while buying and selling items online and in their campus community. It would be the perfect accessory for anyone's college experience.

While amazing progress has been made, Our goal is to eventually launch worldwide until all college students are aware of our work and how it can keep them safe while maximizing their college experience.
